# Contract Terms — Brown, Valdez and Lucas (V-0061)

## Payment Terms
Standard contracted terms are **Net 60**.

## Expedited Shipping Addendum
For orders flagged as expedited/rush shipments, payment terms shorten to **Net 30** in
exchange for the vendor prioritizing the order ahead of standard-lead-time orders. This is a
standing trade-off term, not a contract violation, and applies automatically whenever an
order is marked expedited.

## Escalation Notes
There is no record of any other approved terms variant for this vendor. An invoice showing
Net 45, for example, is not covered by any clause here and should be treated as a genuine
Terms Conflict.
