# Contract Terms — Ferrell, Jones and Lewis (V-0087)

## Payment Terms
Standard contracted terms are **Net 30**.

## Split-Shipment Billing Practice
Ferrell, Jones and Lewis routinely splits large orders into partial shipments and invoices
each shipment separately under the same PO number, sometimes within the same week and for
similar dollar amounts. This is a documented, pre-approved billing practice for this vendor
(see onboarding notes, 2024-09-01) and should NOT be auto-flagged as a duplicate based on
same-vendor/same-amount/near-date matching alone — verify shipment/tracking numbers differ
before treating it as a true duplicate.

## Escalation Notes
If two invoices reference the identical shipment or tracking number, that IS a genuine
duplicate and should be rejected.
