# Contract Terms — Gibson Ltd (V-0143)

## Quantity Tolerance Clause
Per Section 7.4, Gibson Ltd ships heavy industrial equipment components where minor
over/under-shipment is standard industry practice. A quantity variance of up to **2%** of
the ordered quantity (rounded up to a minimum of 1 unit) is contractually authorized and
should not be treated as a Quantity Mismatch exception.

## Escalation Notes
Variance exceeding the 2% tolerance band is not covered by this clause and should be
escalated as a genuine Quantity Mismatch.
