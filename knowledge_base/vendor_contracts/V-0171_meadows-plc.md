# Contract Terms — Meadows PLC (V-0171)

## Payment Terms
Standard payment terms are **Net 45** from invoice date for all purchase orders.

## Seasonal Volume Addendum (Addendum B)
Per Addendum B, signed 2025-09-12: for any PO issued between October 1 and December 31
(Meadows PLC's peak fulfillment season), payment terms automatically extend to **Net 60**
to accommodate the vendor's seasonal cash-flow needs. This extension applies automatically
and does not require a separate amendment per PO — it is a standing contractual term for
Q4 orders. AP should treat an invoice showing Net 60 against a Q4 PO as contractually
correct, not a terms conflict.

## Escalation Notes
Any payment-terms discrepancy outside the Q4 window should still be treated as a genuine
Terms Conflict and routed for vendor clarification.
