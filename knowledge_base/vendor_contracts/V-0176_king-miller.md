# Contract Terms — King-Miller (V-0176)

## Payment Terms
Standard payment terms are **Net 30** from invoice date.

## Volume Tier Addendum
Per the Master Supply Agreement, Section 4.3: purchase orders exceeding **$1,500,000** in
total value automatically qualify for **Net 60** payment terms, in exchange for King-Miller
guaranteeing priority production-line allocation on large orders. This tier applies
regardless of order date and does not require a separate signed amendment.

## Escalation Notes
If an invoice shows Net 60 terms against a PO under the $1.5M threshold, this is NOT covered
by the volume tier and should be treated as a genuine Terms Conflict requiring vendor
clarification.
