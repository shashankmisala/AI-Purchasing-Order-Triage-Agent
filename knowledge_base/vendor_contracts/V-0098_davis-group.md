# Contract Terms — Davis Group (V-0098)

## Payment Terms
Standard contracted terms are **Net 30**.

## Early Payment Discount
Davis Group offers an early-payment discount of **2/10 Net 30** (2% discount if paid within
10 days; otherwise full amount due within 30 days). Davis Group's own invoicing system
prints "2/10 Net 30" on every invoice regardless of whether AP intends to take the discount.
This is the vendor's standard invoice template, not a unilateral change to the underlying
contract terms, and should not by itself be treated as a Terms Conflict — the base term is
still Net 30, the discount is simply optional.

## Escalation Notes
A genuine conflict would be an invoice showing terms other than "Net 30" or "2/10 Net 30"
(e.g., Net 45 or Net 60), which is not authorized under this contract.
