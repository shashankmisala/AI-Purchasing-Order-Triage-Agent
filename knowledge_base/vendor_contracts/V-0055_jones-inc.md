# Contract Terms — Jones Inc (V-0055)

## Annual Price Adjustment Clause
Per Section 5.2, Jones Inc is contractually entitled to a price adjustment effective every
**January 1**, capped at the prior year's published Producer Price Index change. POs issued
in December against pricing locked before the adjustment, but invoiced after January 1, may
legitimately show the new adjusted price.

## Escalation Notes
This clause only applies to invoices dated on or after January 1 of a given year against POs
issued in the preceding December. Outside that window, treat any price variance as a genuine
Price Variance exception.
