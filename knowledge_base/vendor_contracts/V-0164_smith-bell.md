# Contract Terms — Smith-Bell (V-0164)

## Pricing
This is a **fixed-price agreement**. Smith-Bell's contract explicitly does NOT include any
price-escalation, index-tracking, or volume-discount clause. The PO amount agreed at order
time is binding through delivery.

## Escalation Notes
There is no contractual basis for any deviation between the PO amount and the invoiced
amount under this agreement. Any price variance on a Smith-Bell invoice is a genuine billing
discrepancy (vendor error or system error on their end) and should be treated as a real
Price Variance exception — escalate to the vendor for correction, do not approve as-is.
Smith-Bell has previously corrected billing-system errors within 5 business days when
notified.
