# Contract Terms — Snyder, Campos and Callahan (V-0030)

## Volume Discount Tiers
Per the Volume Discount Agreement, Schedule A:
- Orders under $50,000: list price
- Orders $50,000-$150,000: 3% discount
- Orders over $150,000: 5% discount

An invoice priced lower than the PO's list-price amount, consistent with the applicable
discount tier for that order's size, reflects the contracted discount being applied
correctly — not a billing error.

## Escalation Notes
A discount larger than the tier schedule allows, or an invoice priced *higher* than the PO
amount, is not covered by this agreement and should be treated as a genuine Price Variance
exception.
