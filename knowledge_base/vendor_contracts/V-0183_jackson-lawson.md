# Contract Terms — Jackson-Lawson (V-0183)

## Price Escalation Clause
Jackson-Lawson's pricing is tied to a published steel raw-materials index. Per Section 6.1
of the Indexed Pricing Agreement, invoice pricing may vary from the original PO amount by up
to **8%** in either direction without requiring a change order, reflecting index movement
between PO issuance and shipment. Variance within this band is contractually authorized, not
a billing error.

## Escalation Notes
Any variance **above 8%** exceeds the authorized index-tracking band and should still be
escalated as a genuine Price Variance exception requiring manager review — the index clause
does not cover swings of that size.
