# Contract Terms — Curry Inc (V-0177)

## Quantity Requirements
Curry Inc's contract has **no quantity tolerance clause**. The agreement requires
exact-count delivery matching the PO quantity ordered; this is a strict term Curry Inc
specifically requested due to their own downstream inventory-matching requirements.

## Escalation Notes
Any quantity variance, however small, is a genuine Quantity Mismatch under this contract and
should be escalated normally — there is no contractual basis to wave through a shortfall or
overage from this vendor.
